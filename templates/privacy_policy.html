Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines how the **Smart Feedback Analysis and Visualization System** ("the System," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects the data provided through user feedback. Your privacy is critically important to us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when users submit feedback:

  • Feedback Content: The text submitted by the user.
  • Metadata: The timestamp and a unique, anonymous user ID associated with the submission.
  • Derived Data: The sentiment score and sentiment label (Positive, Negative, Neutral) generated by our analysis model.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used solely for the following business purposes:

  • To **Analyze and Visualize** feedback trends on the administrator dashboard.
  • To **Improve** our products and services by identifying areas of strength and weakness based on sentiment.
  • For internal **Reporting and Statistical** purposes related to system usage.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **do not** share, sell, rent, or trade your personal feedback or derived data with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may disclose information only:

  • If required by **Law** (e.g., a court order or subpoena).
  • To protect the **Security** of the System and our users.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect the information we collect from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at: support@smartanalyzer.com.
